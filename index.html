Welcome to Blackjack

Instructions: At the start of the game you and the dealer will get two cards. One of the dealer's cards will not be visible.

Your goal is to have a higher over number than the dealer; however, if you go over 21, you automatically lose.

If you click "Hit Me!", you will draw another card.

If you click "Stay!", the dealer will continue to draw cards until it either beats you, ties you, or busts (>21).

Notes: Ace is always valued at 1. Jack/Queen/King are valued at 11.
